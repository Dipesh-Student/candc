Business Overview

Lavoro Marketing designs and optimizes customer loyalty programs for retail brands across the country. This LLC is headquartered in Chicago, Illinois, but has locations on the East and West coasts to support our widespread presence and clients. The company was founded five years ago by Hugo Pazzo, an industry expert with over 30 years of industry experience. He realized loyalty programs were an innovative way to attract and maintain a strong customer base. He decided to begin Lavoro Marketing to focus on this concept and develop unique software to help businesses manage better manage customers and their data. In the past three years, Lavoro Marketing has grown by 150%. We attribute that growth to our commitment to identifying new clients and markets and hope to double that within the next two years. Currently, we are seeking an investment of $60,000 to help scale our company and grow our team across the United States. We also aim to use those funds to help continue innovating our software to create exciting and rewarding experiences for our customers.
