What we are

Chokshi and Chokshi LLP has a team of experienced and qualified people which includes Chartered Accountants, Ph.D, CAIIB, MBA, Cost Accountant, CISA, Company Secretary and others providing multi functional services across sectors. What makes it unique is the local expertise, personal attention and friendly work environment and on the other side a comprehensive network, breadth of work and resources of an international organisation. It offers a stable career path with challenging opportunities to Trained Accountants and Graduates. The internship program for aspiring Chartered Accountants provides a thorough professional grounding in various service areas and a self-enhancement program.

Due to its ambitious plans C&C continuously looks for above people with outstanding capabilities who can bring new perspectives and dimensions to emerging trends and scenarios. Interested candidates can submit their detailed resume at hr@chokshiandchokshi.in
