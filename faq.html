What is the duration of CA Programme?

With the introduction of Foundation, after completing Class XII, it only takes four years & 6 Month (Minimum) to become a Charted Accountant.

What are the career opportunities for Chartered Accountants in India?

Chartered Accountancy Course offers the students a dream career in the specialized areas of Accounting, Auditing, Corporate Finance, Corporate Laws, and Taxation.

When will the Articleship commence under new scheme of Intermediate Education, training and examination?

The students are eligible for registration for articleship after passing Group I or Group II of Intermediate and the period of Articleship will be three years.
